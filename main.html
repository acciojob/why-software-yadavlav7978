Why I want to become a developer?

I want to become a developer because coding is my passion. As a developer, I have the ability to create solutions to problems, innovate, and build products that can improve people's lives. I enjoy tackling problem-solving challenges, whether it's building websites, apps, or solving problems with software. Additionally, this field constantly evolving nature of technology means there's always something new to learn, which keeps things exciting. Ultimately, becoming a developer allows me to combine my passion for technology with my desire to make a meaningful impact in the world.
